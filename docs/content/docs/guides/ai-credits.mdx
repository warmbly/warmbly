AI features in Warmbly run on credits. Your plan grants a monthly allowance that refreshes every billing cycle, and you can buy top-up packs that never expire when you need more. This guide covers what each action costs, how the two credit pools work, and how to buy more.

<Callout type="info" title="Who can manage credits">
The credits card lives under **Settings, Billing** and requires the **Manage billing** permission, the same permission that controls the rest of billing. The owner and any role granted billing access can view the balance and buy packs; other members do not see it.
</Callout>

## The two credit pools

Every workspace has two separate credit pools:

- **Monthly allowance.** Granted by your plan and reset to the plan amount at the start of each billing cycle. Unused monthly credits do not roll over, they are replaced by the fresh grant.
- **Purchased credits.** Bought as top-up packs. They never expire and are not touched by the monthly reset, so they carry over cycle to cycle.

When you run an AI action, Warmbly spends your monthly allowance first and only draws on purchased credits once the monthly pool is empty. That way your top-ups are held in reserve for months where you go over the allowance.

## What each action costs

Credits are charged from what each call actually uses. Every action has a flat **minimum** that is reserved up front (this is also what gates an empty balance before any work starts), and after the call finishes the real token usage is priced per model. Small calls cost exactly the minimum; long calls with a lot of context or output pay for what they used.

| Action | Minimum credits |
|--------|-----------------|
| Writing assistant draft | 1 |
| AI assistant step (one turn of the dashboard agent) | 1 per turn |
| Reply draft in the unibox | 2 |
| Contact research run | 2 |
| AI step in an automation | 1 |
| Ask AI branch in an automation (per evaluation) | 1 |
| Switch step with the AI decider in a campaign sequence (per contact; the value decider is free) | 1 |
| Inbox agent handled thread | 5 |
| Web search made by an AI step (only when results are found) | 1 |

How the usage pricing works:

- One credit buys a fixed token budget on the model that served the call: light models (mini and haiku class) price at 1,500 tokens per credit, standard models at 400 tokens per credit. The exact model and token count of every charge appear in the transaction log.
- The overage settle can never fail a result you already received: at worst it drains the balance to zero.
- If an AI action fails because of a provider error, the reserved minimum is refunded automatically. You are never charged for output you did not receive.
- A contact research run that finds nothing is still a successful run and costs its credits. You are paying for the search, not the result.
- Running out mid-action returns a clear "insufficient credits" response before any work starts, so a depleted balance never leaves you half-charged.

## Usage overview and spend controls

The **AI usage & spend controls** card under **Settings, Billing** shows today / this week / this month spend, a 30-day usage chart, and breakdowns by feature and by model (credits, tokens, and call counts). It is also where the spend controls live:

- **Spend limits.** Optional hard caps per calendar day, ISO week, and calendar month. When a limit is reached, AI actions pause with a clear message until the window rolls over. Leave a limit off for no cap.
- **Low-credit reminder.** When the balance drops below your threshold, everyone with the Manage billing permission gets an in-app alert (at most once per day) and the credits views refresh live.
- **Auto top-up.** When enabled, Warmbly buys your chosen pack automatically with the card on file the moment the balance dips below your auto top-up threshold. A monthly purchase maximum bounds how much can ever be auto-billed; purchases appear in the transaction log like any other top-up. Auto top-up needs a saved payment method (the card your subscription bills).

## Monthly allowance by plan

Each plan grants a monthly allowance:

| Plan | Monthly credits |
|------|-----------------|
| Free trial | 50 |
| Starter | 250 |
| Pro | 2,000 |
| Enterprise | 25,000 |

Free-trial workspaces get their allowance once, at the start of the trial. Paid workspaces get a fresh grant on every billing cycle. The exact reset date is shown on the credits card next to your balance.

## Buying top-up packs

Paid workspaces can buy top-up packs at any time. Three fixed sizes are available:

- 500 credits
- 2,000 credits
- 10,000 credits

Open **Settings, Billing**, find the **AI credits** card, and pick a pack. You are taken to Stripe Checkout to pay with the card on file. As soon as the payment settles, the credits land in your purchased pool and the balance on the card updates live.

<Callout type="warn" title="Purchases settle through Stripe">
Credits are added only after Stripe confirms the payment, not the moment you click. If you close the checkout tab before paying, nothing is charged and nothing is granted. A completed purchase is applied exactly once, even if Stripe retries the confirmation.
</Callout>

Top-up purchases require an active paid plan. On the free trial the pack buttons are replaced with a prompt to upgrade first.

## Reading the transaction log

The credits card shows a running log of every grant, purchase, and spend, newest first. Each row shows the activity, the model and token count of the call, the signed credit change (green for grants and purchases, plain for spends), the balance afterward, and when it happened. Every AI spend also records exactly what ran it: the campaign, step, and contact for a switch step; the automation, node, and run for an automation step (including what an Ask AI branch asked); the thread for inbox drafts; and the teammate who triggered manual features like the writing assistant. Use **Load more** to page back through older activity.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do unused monthly credits roll over?**
No. The monthly pool is reset to your plan's allowance each cycle. Buy a top-up pack if you want credits that carry over.

**Do purchased credits expire?**
No. Purchased credits stay on your balance until you spend them, through any number of resets.

**What happens to my credits if I change plans?**
Your monthly allowance follows your new plan from the next billing cycle. Purchased credits are unaffected.

**What happens to purchased credits if I cancel?**
They remain on the ledger. Monthly grants stop once the subscription ends, but any purchased balance you already hold stays until spent.

**Do AI credits apply on a self-hosted install?**
Only when you point it at a paid provider. If you run a free or local model (`AI_PROVIDER=ollama`, or any provider with `AI_FREE=true`), AI actions are not charged and the assistant shows a "free model" notice. See the [deployment guide](/development/deployment-guide/).
